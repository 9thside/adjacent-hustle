What It's Like to Work Here

Built for consultants who
want to actually do the work.

01

Real complexity, not PowerPoint strategy

We get embedded in the work—forensic data models, communication plans, cross-functional workstream management. You won't be asked to fill decks.

02

Contract-first, flexibility-forward

We work on engagement-based contracts. You bring expertise when it's needed, on timelines that respect your life. No performative presenteeism.

03

Direct access to decision-makers

You'll sit across from CFOs and COOs, not middle managers. When you have a recommendation, it gets heard by the person who can act on it.

04

Evidence over instinct

We build the database first. If you love turning people data into structural insight, this is the environment where that skill gets used—not sidelined.

05

Small team, high trust

Adjacent Hustle runs lean by design. That means real ownership, real impact, and a direct relationship with the work you deliver—no layers, no credit dilution.

06

Industries that matter to you

Financial services, healthcare, technology, pharma, education—our clients span sectors. You bring the discipline; we bring the breadth.
